We make choosing your cancer treatment easier.

We are an Agile Team of 5 interns creating a tool to help Watson Oncology patients choose the best treatment for them. We aim to create the most compelling tool possible to help patients understand the side effects of various cancer treatments and make an educated decision on which one is best for them.

We take the best treatment options identified by Watson and visualize the side effects, and allowing the patient to interact with the tool by specifying their tolerance of each side effect.

Our Design Philosophy

Be interactive.

Our visualization should help build trust between patients and Watson, through meaningful interaction design during patients decision process.

Be ready to "listen".

We want our users to feel their opinions are heard and valued by Watson. Our visualization supports patients and physicians to collaborate on choosing their preferred treatments and most concerned adverse effects to compare; Our visualization also listen to users' tolerance level on each adverse effect.

Be understanding.

We understand the stress faced by patients and physicans during treatment selection process. Thus, our visualization aims at minimizing users' efforts and efficient presentation.
